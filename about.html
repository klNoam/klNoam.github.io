What is Coding Club?

Coding club is a club that takes place at the Charles E. Smith Jewish Day school at during Community Time on Thursdays. Coding club is an enviroment where students come to work on the projects they are coding, can ask other students who are good at coding for help with coding those projects, or if a student needs help on their school coding project they have an entire room of experts to help them. Coding Club is just a great enviroment to be in while you are coding (and not while you are coding)!
