What is Coding Club?

Coding club is a club that takes place during Community Time, usually on Thursdays. Coding club is an environment where students come to work on the projects they are coding, can ask other students who are good at coding for help with coding those projects, or if a student needs help on their school coding project they have an entire room of experts to help them. Coding Club is a great environment to be in while you are coding (and also while you aren't coding)!

Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring the joy of coding to the Charles E. Smith Jewish Day School קהילה (community), and create a safe space where people can code their projects and talk about coding freely with their peers.
